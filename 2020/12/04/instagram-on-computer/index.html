How to use Instagram on your computer

December 04, 2020 • Social media

So you want to post that dank meme an instagram but don’t want to bust out your mobile or download the app? I got you covered.

How to Post to Instagram from a Computer: #

In Chrome based browsers: #

  1. Open instagram in your web browser
  2. Log in
  3. Open developer tools
  4. Click the networking tab
  5. Click the three dots in the top left
  6. Hover over “more tools”
  7. Click “network conditions”
  8. Under “User Agent” uncheck “Select Automatically”
  9. Choose a mobile option from the list
  10. Refresh the page!

In Firefox based browsers: #

  1. Open instagram in your web browser
  2. Log in
  3. Click the menu
  4. Click “Web Developer”
  5. Click the “Responsive Design Mode” button (ctrl+shift+M)
