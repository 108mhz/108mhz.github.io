# Homemade Toothpaste 

Ingredients:
 
1 tsp coconut oil
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp hydrogen peroxide (food-grade 3%)
3 drops cinnamon or clove oil

Mix the ingredients above and use as you usually would any other toothpaste.
